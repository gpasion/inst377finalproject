What is Valorant?

Valorant is a 5v5 tactical shooter game developed by Riot Games. Released in 2020, it has gained immense popularity for its strategic gameplay, diverse roster of characters (agents), and competitive scene. In this game, players take on the role of unique agents, each with specific abilities, to achieve objectives and outsmart opponents.

Whether you're planting a spike or securing your team’s victory, Valorant offers a high-stakes environment where skill and strategy are the keys to success.

About This Project

This project is designed to help new players familiarize themselves with Valorant’s agents and gameplay. By exploring our site, users can:

  • Learn about the game and its features
  • Filter and explore agents by their roles
  • Discover abilities and background stories for each agent

Our goal is to provide a seamless and interactive experience for players, making it easier for them to choose their agents and improve their gameplay knowledge.
